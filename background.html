The earliest cameras: The first camera known to history is the camera obscura. Conceptual descriptions of camera obscura can be found in Chinese texts from 400 B.C. and in the writings of Aristotle, around 330 B.C. By roughly 1000 A.D., the concept of a camera obscura was articulated by the Arab scholar Ibn Al-Haytham. The Picture below shows how cameras have evolved.
